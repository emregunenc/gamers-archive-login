Privacy Policy for Gamer's Archive

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

At Gamer's Archive, accessible from your GitHub Pages URL, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Gamer's Archive and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Google Login to access Gamer's Archive, we collect basic information provided by Google OAuth:

  • Email address
  • Name and profile picture

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our gaming archive platform.
  • Authenticate your identity and allow you to save your gaming preferences.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our application to improve the user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is handled securely through Supabase infrastructure. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Log Files

Gamer's Archive follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they use the application. This is a standard part of hosting services' analytics.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at emregunenc@gmail.com.
